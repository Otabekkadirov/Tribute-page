Keanu Reeves

A different kind of celebrity.

Here's time line of Keanu Reeves' life:

  • 1964 - was born in English-American family in Beirut, Lebanon. When Reeves was just 3 years old, his father Samuel walked out on the family
  • 1970 - moved to Toronto, Canada with his mother after his parents got divorced. Reeves attended four high schools by the time he dropped out when he was 17 years old.
  • 1984 - started playing a role in Coca Cola commercials and was a correspondent for the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation (CBC) youth television program "Going Great".
  • 1989 - starred in an American science fiction comedy film "Bill and Ted's Excellent Adventures".
  • 1993 - his best friend River Phoenix died of a drug overdose at the age of 23.
  • 1999 - starred in the critically acclaimed science fiction film "The Matrix", the first instalment in what would become The Matrix franchise.
  • 1999 - On December 24, Reeves' girlfriend, Jennifer Syme, gave birth eight months into her pregnancy to Ava Archer Syme-Reeves, who was stillborn. The couple broke up several weeks later. On April 2, 2001, Syme died in a car accident in Los Angeles.
  • 2005 - played the titular role in "Constantine", an occult detective film, about a man who has the ability to perceive and communicate with half-angels and half-demons. The film was a respectable box office hit, grossing $230 million worldwide from a $100 million budget.
  • 2011 - started Arch motorcycle company with motorcycle designer Gard Hollinger.
  • 2014 - faced critical and commercial success for role in "John Wick"
